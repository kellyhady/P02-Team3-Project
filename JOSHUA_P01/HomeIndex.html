Basic Branding Ideas

What does the company do?

The company provides a platform for new artists to introduce themselves to the world. It also gives an opportunity for them to sell their artwork physically

Who is the company to potential customers?

The company targets artists who wants to promote and use this platform as a service. It also targets art collectors or galleries who wants to buy art to use and decorate their homes/galleries or their own viewing pleasure.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

because in a world with many digital arts being made there are still a lot of new talents around the world that would like to paint or draw with their own fingers. Art galleries still garners many people's attention despite people moving on to a more modern medium for art.
